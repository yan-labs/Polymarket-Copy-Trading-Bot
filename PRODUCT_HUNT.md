## Detailed Description (500 chars max)

PolyCopy is the easiest way to copy trade on Polymarket — the world's largest prediction market.

**How it works:**
1. Connect your Polygon wallet
2. Browse and select top traders to follow
3. Set your position size and risk parameters
4. Let PolyCopy automatically copy their trades

**Why PolyCopy?**
- Real-time trade detection (seconds, not minutes)
- Smart position sizing based on your balance
- Follow multiple traders simultaneously
- Full analytics and trade history
- 7-day free trial — no credit card needed

Perfect for busy professionals who want to profit from prediction markets without watching charts all day.

## Comment Templates

### For "What's your pricing?" questions:
> We have 4 plans: Free (1 trader), Basic $29/mo (5 traders), Pro $59/mo (20 traders), and Enterprise $99/mo (100 traders). All plans include a 7-day free trial of Pro features!

### For "How is this different from MirrorCopy?" questions:
> Great question! We're focused on simplicity and value. Our Pro plan ($59/mo) gives you 20 traders vs MirrorCopy's Growth plan ($79/mo) with 10 wallets. Plus our setup is simpler — just connect wallet and go. We're newer, so we're hungry for feedback!

### For "Is my money safe?" questions:
> Great question about security! PolyCopy is non-custodial — we never hold your funds. You just provide your wallet's private key for signing trades (encrypted with AES-256). All funds stay in your wallet, and you can revoke access anytime.
